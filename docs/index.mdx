Welcome to Yappa! This is the place where you can find all the information you need to get the most out of Yappa.

## Overview

Yappa is a productivity tool that helps you track and manage your work across calendars, tasks, and projects. It's designed to help you stay organized as you acquire new clients and grow your business.

### Concepts

Yappa is built around the following concepts:

* Calendar Accounts and Calendars
* Calendar View
* Time Tracking Events
* Calendar Synchronizations
* Projects and Clients
* Booking Links
